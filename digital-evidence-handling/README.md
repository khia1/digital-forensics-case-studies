# Digital Evidence Handling and Integrity

## Scenario
An investigation was conducted to demonstrate proper handling and verification of digital evidence in accordance with forensic standards.

## Tools Used
FTK Imager  
Hash verification tools  

## Investigation Process
1. Collected digital evidence from storage media.
2. Generated MD5 and SHA hashes to verify integrity.
3. Documented chain of custody procedures.
4. Ensured evidence preservation and admissibility.

## Key Findings
Proper hashing and documentation confirmed that the digital evidence remained unchanged throughout the investigation process.

## Skills Demonstrated
Evidence acquisition  
Chain of custody documentation  
Hash verification  
Digital forensic procedures
